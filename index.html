Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Globe Quiz 3D is a geography quiz application available on iOS and Android. This privacy policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Data We Collect

Local storage (on your device only)

  • Quiz records and scores
  • Premium status
  • App preferences

This data is stored locally using Capacitor Preferences and is never transmitted to our servers. We have no backend and do not collect this data.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data

AdMob uses this data to serve relevant ads. You can learn more at Google Privacy Policy.

In-app purchases (RevenueCat)

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Purchase history and transaction identifiers
  • Device and platform information

No payment details are processed by us or RevenueCat — all payments are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play. Learn more at RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

2. Third-Party Services

The app may open Wikipedia pages in an in-app browser when you tap on a country or capital. Wikipedia's own privacy policy applies in that context: Wikimedia Privacy Policy.

3. Children's Privacy

Globe Quiz 3D is a geography educational app suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Advertising shown in the app complies with applicable regulations regarding child-directed content.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

All locally stored data (scores, preferences) can be deleted by uninstalling the app. For data collected by third-party services (AdMob, RevenueCat), please refer to their respective privacy policies.

To request deletion of any data associated with your use of the app, contact us at the email below.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
